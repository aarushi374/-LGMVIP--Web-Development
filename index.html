What are the benefits of having my pet sprayed or neutered?

Your female pet will live a longer, healthier life. Neutering your male companion prevents testicular cancer and some prostate problems.

Yes behaviour tests were conducted on all our pets

All our pets are checked for heatworms,given all the necessary vaccines and thoroughly examined for any additional medical problems.
